Our Story

Trinidad's beloved independent boutique hotel.

Conceived as a family restaurant in 1964, Kapok has grown into a 95-room boutique hotel that remains proudly locally-owned and independently operated. We sit just outside the capital, in the leafy suburb of St. Clair.

All within safe, walking distance to the Queen's Park Savannah — home to Trinidad's famous Carnival — along with the Magnificent Seven, the Emperor Valley Zoo and the Botanical Gardens.

Beyond the Hotel

Trinidad Experiences.

Discover the island beyond our doors. From ancient volcanic mud baths to pristine Caribbean beaches and rare wildlife sanctuaries — Trinidad offers adventures unlike anywhere else in the world. Our Front Desk can arrange bookings for all excursions below.

Enquire at Front Desk →
🌋
Featured · Natural Wonder

Mud Volcano
at Piparo

One of the world's most accessible active mud volcanoes. Located in central Trinidad, the Piparo mud volcano erupts cool grey mud creating a surreal, otherworldly landscape. A geological marvel found nowhere else in the Caribbean.

⏱ ~4–5 hrs
🚗 ~45 min from hotel
👥 All ages
Book via Front Desk →
🦜
Wildlife · UNESCO Listed ~3 hrs

Caroni Swamp Bird Sanctuary

Glide through the mangroves at sunset as thousands of Scarlet Ibis — Trinidad's national bird — return to roost in a breathtaking crimson spectacle. A UNESCO-recognised wetland and one of the most extraordinary wildlife experiences in the Caribbean.

🌅 Sunset tours 🚤 Boat tour 📍 ~20 min south

Nature · Birding

Asa Wright Nature Centre

A world-renowned tropical birding sanctuary in the rainforest of the Northern Range. Over 400 species recorded, including the rare Oilbird in its natural cave habitat.

⏱ Full day  ·  🌿 Guided tours available
Arrange via Front Desk →
🌴
Beach · Food

Maracas Bay

Trinidad's most iconic beach, framed by lush mountains. Famous for its bake and shark vendors, crystal-clear waves, and the winding scenic drive through the Northern Range to get there.

⏱ Full day  ·  🚐 ~45 min from hotel
Arrange via Front Desk →
⚫
Geology · Unique

Pitch Lake, La Brea

The world's largest natural deposit of asphalt — a UNESCO Global Geosite covering 40 hectares. You can actually walk on it. A truly unique geological phenomenon found only in Trinidad.

⏱ Half day  ·  🚗 ~1.5 hrs south
Arrange via Front Desk →

Explore the capital's rich colonial heritage — the Red House parliament, National Museum, the bustling markets of downtown POS and the vibrant street art of Woodbrook. All walking distance or a short ride.
